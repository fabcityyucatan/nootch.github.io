Giving tools to enhance the environment.

Nootch is a kit that helps citizens and cities to create their own data in order to create an open envirnomental data enhanced by citizens.

AIR QUALITY

Measuring dust particles like PM1.0, PM2.5 and PM10. Also organic gases like TVOC and eCO2.

WEATHER CONDITIONS

Measuring levels of temperature (Cº), humidity (%) and barometric pressure (KPa).

UV RADIATION

Measuring UVA and UVB (nm) and also UV Index levels.

NOISE LEVELS

Measuring levels of acoustic pollution (dBA).

Tools for a change

Technology to empower communities.

Nootch will provide benefits at different levels:

For science

Inspires new research topics, fosters multidisciplinary jobs, create large data sets that allow more in-depth investigations, increase public acceptance of results and promote their evaluation.

For society

Promotes socially relevant research, allows the co-creation of transparent research, democratizes the meaning of science, strengthens civil society, develops opportunities for social transformation.

For cities

It allows contributing to scientific research, improves the understanding of science, fosters interest in scientific activities and topics, facilitates their participation in political decision-making, works for a better environment and a better society.
